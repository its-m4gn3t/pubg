Digital Nepal 2030 🇳🇵

Transforming Nepal into a $1B+ Digital Economy

🌍 Vision

Nepal will transition from a remittance-based economy to a Digital Export Economy powered by AI, IT services, and data infrastructure.

🚨 Current Challenge

Low ICT Budget (<1%)
Brain Drain of Youth
Underutilized Hydropower
Weak Execution Systems

🎯 Strategic Goal

Generate NPR 100 अरब+ annually within 5–7 years through digital exports.

🧩 Core Pillars

💻 Data Centers & AI Infrastructure

Build hyperscale data centers using hydropower.

Revenue: 30–40 अरब/year

🌐 IT Export Economy

Global freelancing + outsourcing hub.

Revenue: 20–30 अरब/year

🔗 Digital ID & API Economy

Monetize government APIs and digital identity.

Revenue: 10–15 अरब/year

🏔️ Smart Tourism

Increase tourist spending via digital platforms.

Revenue: 10–20 अरब/year

🧠 AI Made in Nepal

Develop Nepali AI models for national use.

💰 Budget & Revenue Plan

Sector Investment Annual Revenue
Data Centers 50 अरब 40 अरब
IT Export 10 अरब 30 अरब
Digital APIs 8 अरब 15 अरब
Tourism Tech 5 अरब 15 अरब

🏗️ Funding Model

PPP Investment
Foreign Direct Investment

⚖️ Terms & Conditions

✔ Blockchain-based transparency
✔ KPI-based fund release
✔ Mandatory PPP for large projects
✔ Export-focused incentives
✔ Strict deadlines (12-month review)

⏱️ Timeline

Year 1–2: Setup & Policy

Year 3–5: Scale & Growth

Year 5–7: 100 अरब+ Revenue Achieved

🚀 Conclusion

Nepal can become South Asia’s next digital hub by focusing on execution, transparency, and export-driven technology.
